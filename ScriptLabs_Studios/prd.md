# 🚀 ScriptLabs MVP Website - Product Requirements Document
*Version 1.0 | Development Studio & AI-Powered SaaS Platform*

## Executive Summary

**Project:** ScriptLabs Company Website MVP  
**Objective:** Launch a professional website showcasing our development studio capabilities, proprietary AI technology, and SaaS platform offerings  
**Timeline:** 8-12 weeks  
**Budget:** $25,000 - $40,000  
**Target Audience:** Startups, scale-ups, enterprise clients seeking web/mobile/Web3 development with AI integration  

## Product Vision

**Mission Statement:**
Create a cutting-edge website that positions ScriptLabs as the premier development studio for AI-powered applications across web, mobile, and Web3 platforms, demonstrating our ability to create solutions "out of thin air."

**Key Value Propositions:**
1. **Proprietary AI Integration** - Showcase our custom algorithms and AI capabilities
2. **Multi-Platform Expertise** - Demonstrate web, mobile, and Web3 development skills  
3. **SaaS Platform Specialization** - Highlight enterprise-grade scalable solutions
4. **Innovation Leadership** - Position as technology pioneers and thought leaders
5. **Rapid Development** - Emphasize speed and efficiency in project delivery

## Technical Specifications

### Performance Requirements
- **Page Load Speed:** <3 seconds on desktop, <4 seconds on mobile
- **Core Web Vitals:** All metrics in "Good" range
- **Uptime:** 99.9% availability SLA
- **Mobile Performance:** 90+ Google PageSpeed score
- **Progressive Web App:** Offline capability for key pages

### SEO Strategy
- **Target Keywords:** AI development, custom software, Web3 development, SaaS platforms
- **Content Calendar:** 2-3 technical blog posts per week
- **Local SEO:** Target major tech hubs (SF, NYC, Austin, Seattle, Boston)
- **Technical SEO:** Schema markup, XML sitemaps, structured data
- **Link Building:** Industry partnerships and guest posting strategy

### Security & Compliance
- **SSL Certificate:** Extended validation certificate
- **Privacy Compliance:** GDPR, CCPA, and other regional requirements
- **Data Encryption:** All form submissions and data transmission
- **Accessibility:** WCAG 2.1 AA compliance
- **Vulnerability Scanning:** Regular security assessments

### Integration Requirements
- **CRM Integration:** HubSpot or Salesforce for lead management
- **Email Marketing:** Automated nurture sequences and newsletters
- **Calendar Booking:** Calendly or similar for consultation scheduling
- **Analytics Suite:** Google Analytics 4, Google Tag Manager
- **Chat System:** Intercom or custom AI chat integration
- **Payment Processing:** Stripe for consultation fees and deposits
